# CC2.1 COSO Principle 13: The entity obtains or generates and uses relevant, quality information to support the functioning of internal control
Identifies Information Requirements - A process is in place to identify the information required and expected to support the functioning of the other components of internal control and the achievement of the entityâ€™s objectives. Captures Internal and External Sources of Data - Information systems capture internal and external sources of data. Processes Relevant Data Into Information - Information systems process and transform relevant data into information. Maintains Quality Throughout Processing - Information systems produce information that is timely, current, accurate, complete, accessible, protected, verifiable, and retained. Information is reviewed to assess its relevance in supporting the internal control components.
